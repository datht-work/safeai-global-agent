# Americas Privacy & Cybersecurity Regulations

This document outlines the core data protection and cybersecurity regulations for the Americas region, used by SafeAI-Global.

## United States (US - Federal)

- **Applicable Regulations**: COPPA 2025 Amendments, NIST AI RMF, HIPAA (Healthcare), GLBA (Financial), FTC Act, Bulk Data Rule 2025
- **Cybersecurity & Resilience (by 2026)**:
  - **SEC Cybersecurity Rules**: Publicly traded companies must disclose material cybersecurity incidents within 4 business days. Mandatory annual disclosures regarding cybersecurity risk management, strategy, and board oversight. Smaller reporting companies must comply by June 2026.
- **AI Governance**: Govern → Map → Measure → Manage lifecycle under NIST AI RMF.

## United States (US - State Level)

- **California (CA)**: CCPA/CPRA, California Delete Act (DROP 2026), CA AI Transparency Act 2026, CalOPPA
- **Colorado (CO)**: CPA (Colorado Privacy Act), Colorado AI Act 2026
- **Texas (TX)**: TDPSA (Texas Data Privacy & Security Act), TX Responsible AI Governance Act 2026
- **Virginia (VA)**: VCDPA (Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act)
- **New York (NY)**: SHIELD Act, DFS Cybersecurity Regulation (23 NYCRR 500)
- **Cross-Border Transfer**: Varies by state; contractual data protection addendum recommended based on state laws.

## Canada (CA)

- **Applicable Regulations**: PIPEDA, Quebec Law 25, CPPA (proposed), AIDA (Artificial Intelligence & Data Act)
- **AI Governance**: High-impact AI assessment, bias mitigation under proposed AIDA.

## Brazil (BR)

- **Applicable Regulations**: LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados), AI Regulatory Framework (PL 2338/2023), Digital Child and Adolescent Statute (Digital ECA) 2026
- **Cross-Border Transfer**: LGPD Art. 33 requires adequacy, SCCs, BCRs, or specific consent.
- **AI Governance**: Risk-based approach, human oversight for high-risk AI under PL 2338/2023. Prohibition of Loot Boxes under Digital ECA.

## Mexico (MX)

- **Applicable Regulations**: LFPDPPP (Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data), NOM-151

## Argentina (AR)

- **Applicable Regulations**: Personal Data Protection Law 25.326, AAIP Regulations

## Colombia (CO)

- **Applicable Regulations**: Law 1581/2012 (Habeas Data), Decree 1377/2013

## Peru (PE)

- **Applicable Regulations**: Personal Data Protection Law 29733 (2025 Amendments)
